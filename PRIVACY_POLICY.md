# Privacy Policy for STAR SHARE

Last updated: June 08, 2026

STAR SHARE (the "Application") is developed by Aref Bahrami (operating under the name "SmallCreators") as a free utility application. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for users of our Application on Android and Windows platforms.

By downloading and using STAR SHARE, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

STAR SHARE is built as a **fully offline, peer-to-peer (P2P)** local network file transfer and communication tool. 

* **No Data Collection:** We do **NOT** collect, harvest, store, or transmit any of your personal data, files, photos, contact lists, or chat messages to any external or remote servers.
* **Local Processing Only:** All data transmission (such as file sharing and chat messages) occurs exclusively on your local private network (LAN) or mobile hotspot. The data goes directly from your sending device to the receiving device without passing through any third-party intermediary or cloud infrastructure.

---

## 2. Permissions Used and Why

To perform its core functions of local file sharing and communication, the Application requires the following system permissions:

* **Local Network / Wi-Fi Multicast State / Internet:** Required solely to scan the local subnet, discover other local devices running the Application, and transmit files/chats peer-to-peer within the same network. No data leaves your local network.
* **Storage / Public Downloads Folder Access:** Required to save received files directly to your system's public Downloads directory. The Application does not read or access any other files on your device.

---

## 3. Local Chat Security and Privacy

The Application includes an offline local network chat room:
* **Private / Locked Messages:** Messages can be locked using a user-defined 4-digit PIN. These messages are only readable on devices with the matching PIN. For other devices, they are shown masked as `****`.
* **Public Messages:** Viewable by anyone on the local network. Users can disable default public message visibility in settings.
* **No Storage:** Chat history is kept in the temporary on-screen memory and is not archived or uploaded to any database. Once the app is closed, local chats are cleared.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

Since STAR SHARE does not collect, store, or transmit any user data or personal information of any kind, the Application is completely safe for children of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from anyone.

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking libraries, or advertising networks (SDKs) that collect user metrics or track your behavior.
